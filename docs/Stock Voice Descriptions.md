### **Design Tips for your Avatars:**
*   **Lighting:** For "Professional" voices (Leda, Callirrhoe, Zubenelgenubi), use high-contrast, three-point studio lighting. For "Casual" voices (Schedar, Iapetus, Puck), use natural, diffused window light.
*   **Color Palette:**
    *   **Trust/Authority:** Navy blues, grays, and whites.
    *   **Approachability/Friendly:** Warm yellows, oranges, and soft greens.
    *   **Youth/Energy:** Bright cyans, hot pinks, or saturated primary colors.
*   **Framing:** Use "Head and Shoulders" framing for all to keep the focus on the persona's expression, ensuring the eyes—the primary point of connection—are sharp and well-lit.
